F.A.Q

Is there a travel city rewards program?

Yes, Travel City offers a rewards program where you can earn points for bookings and redeem them for discounts on future trips.

Can I change or cancel my bookings?

Yes, you can change or cancel your bookings based on the policy of the service provider. Please check the specific terms and conditions of your booking.

Does Travel City offer insurance?

Yes, Travel City offers various travel insurance options to ensure your trips are protected. You can select the insurance plan that best suits your needs during the booking process.

How do I contact Travel City customer service?

You can contact Travel City customer service via phone, email, or live chat on our website. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries.

How do I book flights or hotels on Travel City?

Booking flights or hotels on Travel City is easy. Just search for your destination, select your preferred options, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your booking.
